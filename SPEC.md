# ICT Pulse - Institutional Trading Dashboard

## Core ICT Concepts & Methodology

### Kill Zones (High Probability Trading Sessions)
1. **London Kill Zone**: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM GMT (2:00 AM - 5:00 AM EST)
2. **New York Kill Zone**: 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM EST (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM GMT)
3. **Asia Kill Zone**: 12:00 AM - 3:00 AM GMT (7:00 PM - 10:00 PM EST)

### ICT Models
1. **Money Block Model** - Accumulation/distribution zones
2. **Kill Zone Model** - Session-based momentum
3. **Order Blinding Model** - Institutional order clusters
4. **Liquidity Pool Model** - Stop hunts above/below
5. **Silver Bullet Model** - High-probability entries
6. **2:00 AM Fade Model** - Counter-trend at 2:00 AM EST
7. **Open Auction Model** - First 15-30 min of session
8. **High Tide/Low Tide** - Range validation
9. **Bull/Bear Flag** - Continuation patterns
10. **Equal Highs/Lows** - Double top/bottom patterns

### Key ICT Concepts
- **MSS (Market Structure Shift)**: Trend change confirmation
- **BOS (Break of Structure)**: Higher high/lower low break
- **Order Blocks**: Institutional order zones
- **FVG (Fair Value Gap)**: Imbalance zones
- **OTE (Optimal Trade Entry)**: Fibonacci-based entries
- **Fairest Price**: Mid-range of daily candle
- **Inducement**: Fake breakouts to trap retail
- **Premium/Discount**: Above/below daily mid-point
- **Displacement**: Strong candle closes beyond structure
- **9:50 AM Reversal**: NY session specific reversal
- **10:00 AM Reversal**: NY session closing pattern
